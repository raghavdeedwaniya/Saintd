Who We Are

Building Tomorrow's Leaders, Today

Saint "D" Public School is one of Mandal's most respected educational institutions, proudly affiliated with the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE). We offer comprehensive schooling from Play Group to Class 10.

"Our mission is simple — every student who walks through our gates will walk out with the knowledge, character, and confidence to succeed in life."

Located near the Government Hospital on Dhovani Nadi Road, Mandal, our campus is equipped with modern classrooms, a fully supervised hostel, and a caring team of educators who treat every student as their own.
