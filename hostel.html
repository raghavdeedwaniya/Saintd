Our Hostel
Where Students Thrive

Saint "D" Public School offers modern hostel facilities for students coming from villages and cities outside Mandal. Our hostel is designed to give students the best environment to focus on studies while staying comfortable and safe.

  • ❄️

    Air-Conditioned Rooms

    All hostel rooms are fully air-conditioned for comfort throughout the year.

  • 📹

    24/7 CCTV Surveillance

    The entire hostel is monitored round the clock by high-resolution CCTV cameras.

  • 🍽️

    Nutritious Meals

    Healthy, home-cooked meals provided three times a day, including fresh breakfast, lunch and dinner.

  • 📚

    Dedicated Study Hours

    Structured evening study time with teacher supervision for focused academic work.

  • 🏥

    Near Government Hospital

    Our campus is located next to the Government Hospital for immediate medical access if needed.
